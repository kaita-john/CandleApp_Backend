StarPal Safety Standards

StarPal Commitment to Protecting Children and Ensuring a Safe Platform

Introduction

At StarPal, the safety and well-being of our users, especially children, are of utmost importance. We are committed to protecting children from exploitation, abuse, and harmful content. Our policies are designed to create a safe and secure environment for everyone on our platform.

Prohibited Content and Activities

We strictly prohibit the following content and activities on StarPal:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form.
  • Sexual exploitation of minors, including grooming and solicitation.
  • Harassment, bullying, or abuse targeted at minors.
  • Sharing or promoting inappropriate or harmful content involving children.
  • Misrepresentation of age or identity to engage with minors.

Preventative Measures

To uphold these standards, we implement the following measures:

  • User Verification: We use verification mechanisms to confirm the identity of users, ensuring compliance with our age requirements.
  • Content Moderation: Our advanced AI systems and dedicated moderation team actively monitor for inappropriate or harmful content.
  • Reporting Mechanisms: Users can easily report violations or suspicious behavior via in-app tools.
  • Parental Controls: Features are available to restrict access to age-inappropriate content.
  • Regular Training: Our team undergoes regular training to detect and address potential CSAE cases.

Reporting and Assistance

If you encounter any behavior or content that violates our policies, we encourage you to report it immediately. Our support team is available to address your concerns and take prompt action.

Collaboration with Authorities

StarPal collaborates with law enforcement and child protection organizations to address cases of child exploitation swiftly. We comply with applicable laws and promptly report CSAE incidents.
